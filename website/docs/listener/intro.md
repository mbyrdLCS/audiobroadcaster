# Listener Guide

Audio Broadcaster lets you hear the church service audio through your phone — useful if you're hard of hearing, sitting in a noisy area, or want to follow along in another language.

## What You Need

- Your phone (iPhone or Android)
- Connected to the church Wi-Fi
- The B1 app installed

That's it — no other app or setup required.
