# Troubleshooting (Listeners)

## I can't hear anything

- Make sure your phone volume is turned up
- **iPhone users:** Check the silent switch on the left side of your phone — flip it so no orange is showing
- Make sure you tapped **Play**
- Try closing the page and opening it again from B1

## The page won't load

- Check that you're connected to the **church Wi-Fi** — not your mobile data or a guest network
- Ask someone at the church if Audio Broadcaster is running on the Mac
- Try refreshing the page

## The audio cuts out or sounds choppy

- Move closer to a Wi-Fi access point if you can
- Close other apps running in the background on your phone
- Try leaving and rejoining the listener page

## Translation isn't working

- Make sure you selected a language from the dropdown
- On iPhone, tap the **"Tap to Enable Voice"** button after selecting a language
- If it still doesn't work, the translation service may be temporarily unavailable — switch back to original audio

## I hear an echo or feedback

This usually happens if the phone is near the speakers or microphone being used to broadcast. Move further away or use headphones.

## Still need help?

Ask someone at the welcome desk or contact the church office.
