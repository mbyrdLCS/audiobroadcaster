# First Run Checklist

Run through this before your first service to make sure everything is working.

## Before the Service

- [ ] Mac is connected to church Wi-Fi (or ethernet on the same network)
- [ ] Audio source is connected (mixer, interface, or mic)
- [ ] Audio Broadcaster is open and showing the listener URL
- [ ] Correct audio input is selected in the app
- [ ] Audio level meter is moving when someone speaks
- [ ] Listener URL in B1 matches what the app shows

## Test With a Phone

1. On a phone connected to church Wi-Fi, open the B1 app
2. Tap the **Listen Live** tab you added
3. Tap **Play**
4. Confirm you can hear audio on the phone

## During the Service

- Leave Audio Broadcaster open on the Mac — closing it stops the stream
- The app runs in the background; you don't need to interact with it
- If you restart the Mac between services, reopen the app and verify the listener URL hasn't changed

## After the Service

You can leave the app running or close it — it has no effect on anything else. There's nothing to save or shut down.
